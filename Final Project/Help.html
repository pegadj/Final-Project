How to play?

1

A sequence of buttons will light up on the screen.

2

Repeat the Sequence

Enter the sequence correctly. Each successful round adds an extra step.

3

Avoid Mistakes

Incorrect inputs will reset the progress and end the game.

The game defaults to normal mode, but you can switch to Color, Numbers, or Letter mode.
